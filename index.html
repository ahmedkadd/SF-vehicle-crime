Project narrative

This project will be examining crime in San Francisco. Car break-ins are a common crime that happens in the city. I will be using police report data to create visualizations that will examine the problem of car break-ins. I would like to answer the question of where are the safest and most dangerous places to park your car in San Francisco. I would also like to examine the number of car break-ins over time to see if the problem has been getting worse.
